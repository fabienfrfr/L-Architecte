# System Intent & Stakeholders

## Mission Statement

To provide an automated, local-first agentic pipeline that transforms raw business ideas into production-ready, SOLID-compliant software artifacts.

## Stakeholders (Interactors)

* **The Architect (User)**: Defines the vision and validates the output.
* **Ollama (LLM Engine)**: Provides the local intelligence.
* **GPU Resources**: The physical constraint for inference performance.
* **File System**: The persistent storage for generated code, ADRs, and diagrams.
